# TASK-1--FUTURE_DS_01
# Superstore Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as Task 1 during my Data Analytics Internship at Future Interns.

The project focuses on analyzing Superstore sales data to understand business performance in terms of revenue, profit, customer segments, product categories, and regional trends. The aim of this project is to extract meaningful insights from the data and provide practical recommendations that can help improve business decision-making and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Evaluate regional sales distribution
* Track monthly sales and profit trends
* Understand customer segment contributions
* Generate actionable business insights and recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

This project was implemented using:

* Microsoft Excel (Data Preparation)
* Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboard Development)

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Superstore Sales Dataset**

The dataset contains:

* Order details
* Customer information
* Product categories and sub-categories
* Sales and profit data
* Regional information
* Shipping details

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

The analysis includes:

* Data cleaning and preparation
* KPI calculation and performance tracking
* Monthly sales and profit trend analysis
* Regional sales and profit evaluation
* Category and sub-category performance analysis
* Customer segment analysis
* Sales distribution by shipping mode
* Geographic sales visualization using maps

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* The West region generated the highest sales performance.
* Technology emerged as the highest revenue and profit-generating category.
* Canon ImageCLASS 2200 was identified as the top-performing product.
* Consumer customers contributed the largest share of sales.
* Standard Class shipping accounted for the highest sales volume.
* Sales experienced significant growth during the final quarter of the year.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

* Sales, Profit, Orders, and Profit Margin KPIs
* Monthly Sales & Profit Trend Analysis
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Customer Segment
* Sales by Product Category

* Sales by State (Map Visualization)
